R Packages

In an effort to promote collaboration on analytical projects and facilitate code replicability, we've developed R Packages and encourage the use of git / github as a version control tool. These packages are primarily used and maintained by OHA/HQ staff but remain accessible to other analysts across the bureau, the agency, abroad.

We will continue to build and promote similar tools to strengthen our ability to quickly respond to decision makers, and enhance staff ability to work with HIV/AIDS and other public health related datasets.
